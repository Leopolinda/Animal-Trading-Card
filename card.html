Axolotl

Axolotl is known for its unique ability to regenerate body parts, including the heart and brain.

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Average Length: 15-45 centimeters
  • Averafe Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Xochimilco Lake, Mexico City

Most commonly pale pink or black with black eyes and a wide head, Axolotls are an interesting animal. They are carnivorse that feed primerly on worms, insects and small fish. They reach about 45cm in lenght and live up to 15 years, some even longer depending on their habitat.
